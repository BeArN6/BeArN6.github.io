The Nuclear Security
Working Group (NSWG)
is a non-partisan group of
experts working to promote
discourse and build
consensus on the United
States’ most pressing
nuclear issues.

We work behind the scenes to provide
policymakers and NGOs with the
expertise they need to make informed
decisions on global nuclear issues.

Our leadership team includes senior
foreign policy experts, military officials,
and leading scholars in the field of
nuclear security.
